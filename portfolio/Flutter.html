Daily Catholic Saints

I developed the Daily Saints App, a spiritually enriching mobile application designed for daily reflection on the lives of Catholic saints. The app features inspiring texts, beautiful imagery, and an integrated podcast player powered by the Spreaker API. It supports offline access using SQLite and utilizes BLoC architecture for efficient state management. With customizable themes and font settings, the app combines modern technology with timeless faith to support users in their daily spiritual journey.
